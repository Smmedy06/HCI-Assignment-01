Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our programs

What age groups do you cater to?
+

We offer programs for children ages 4-16, with specialized curricula designed for different developmental stages. Our courses are structured to match learning capabilities and interests of each age group.

Are the classes conducted online or in-person?
+

We offer both online and hybrid learning options. Our interactive online platform provides engaging virtual classrooms, while our hybrid programs combine online learning with hands-on activities at home.

How do you track my child's progress?
+

Parents receive weekly progress reports, access to our parent dashboard with real-time updates, and monthly one-on-one consultations with teachers to discuss your child's achievements and areas for improvement.

What if my child needs extra support in certain subjects?
+

We provide personalized learning paths and one-on-one tutoring sessions when needed. Our teachers identify learning gaps early and provide targeted support to ensure every child succeeds.
